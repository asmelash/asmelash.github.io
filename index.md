# Edurest

## Scaling the Peak of Fluid Dynamics and Sustainable Energy Systems
*Bridging Academic Research with High-Performance Technology*

Welcome to **Edurest**, a digital laboratory dedicated to the intersection of **Education, Research, and Technology**. 

As a researcher specializing in **Mechanical Engineering and Thermofluid Sciences**, my work focuses on using computational power to solve real-world energy challenges. From mastering complex CFD simulations in **OpenFOAM** to designing **Hybrid Renewable Energy Systems**, Edurest serves as a hub for sharing technical insights, teaching foundational engineering principles, and documenting the journey of innovation.
