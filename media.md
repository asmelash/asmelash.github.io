# CFD Simulation Visualizations

This page presents selected flow visualizations from my research on wind turbine aerodynamics and moving-boundary fluid mechanics. The simulations illustrate unsteady flow structures, wake development, and vortex dynamics associated with different turbine concepts and numerical approaches. Additional unpublished simulations are intentionally kept private.

---

## Vertical-Axis Wind Turbines (VAWT)

### Two-Blade VAWT – Unsteady Flow Structures

[![Two-blade VAWT CFD simulation](https://img.youtube.com/vi/NXwFNuot1Qo/hqdefault.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NXwFNuot1Qo)

*Numerical simulation of a two-blade vertical-axis wind turbine illustrating blade rotation, wake development, and vorticity distribution. The visualization highlights unsteady aerodynamic behavior and vortex shedding in the near wake.*

---

### Three-Blade VAWT – Wake Development

[![VAWT CFD simulation](https://img.youtube.com/vi/rtQMsZM5km0/hqdefault.jpg)](https://youtu.be/rtQMsZM5km0)

*Unsteady flow visualization showing wake evolution and vortex structures around a three-blade vertical-axis wind turbine under rotating motion.*

---

## Track-Based Vertical-Axis Turbines (TVAT)

### Single-Blade Track-Based Turbine – Multiple Trajectories

[![Track-based wind turbine CFD simulation](https://img.youtube.com/vi/uI6rPAVYp2Q/hqdefault.jpg)](https://youtu.be/uI6rPAVYp2Q)

*Simulation of a track-based vertical-axis wind turbine with a single blade following different predefined trajectories. The flow field illustrates wake formation, blade–wake interaction, and the influence of kinematic paths on aerodynamic behavior.*

---

### Two-Blade Track-Based Turbine – Mixed Kinematics

[![Track-based VAWT mixed kinematics](https://img.youtube.com/vi/7MEAo2nXUsM/hqdefault.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7MEAo2nXUsM)

*Unsteady flow visualization of a two-blade track-based vertical-axis turbine following mixed circular and oval trajectories. The results highlight complex vorticity structures and wake–blade interactions induced by non-circular motion.*

---

## Horizontal-Axis Wind Turbines (HAWT)

### Full-Scale HAWT – Flow Field Visualization

[![HAWT flow field simulation](https://img.youtube.com/vi/qQhKXk9FkTo/hqdefault.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qQhKXk9FkTo)

*Experimental and full-scale flow-field visualization of a horizontal-axis wind turbine, illustrating large-scale wake structures and aerodynamic interaction with the incoming flow.*

---

## Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics (SPH)

### Flow Around Oscillating Cylinders

[![SPH oscillating cylinders](https://img.youtube.com/vi/zdWGLh70Xa4/hqdefault.jpg)](https://youtu.be/zdWGLh70Xa4)

*Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics simulation of flow around oscillating cylinders, highlighting vortex shedding, unsteady wake dynamics, and particle-based representation of complex flow motion.*
