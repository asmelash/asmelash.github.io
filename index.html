Mumbai, India

Research Focus

My research centers on high-fidelity computational fluid dynamics (CFD) for aerospace, mechanical, and wind/renewable energy systems. I specialize in turbulence modeling, moving-geometry simulations (sliding and overset meshes), and aerodynamic analysis of unconventional wind turbines using OpenFOAM and high-performance computing workflows.

Selected Projects & Simulations

Track-Based Vertical-Axis Wind Turbines

Developed CFD frameworks using sliding and overset mesh techniques. Optimization of blade trajectories using URANS-based flow analysis.

VAWT Aerodynamics & Wake Analysis

Simulation illustrating wake development and unsteady flow features of a three-blade Darrieus-type turbine configuration.

Horizontal-Axis Wind Turbine (HAWT)

Studied rotor performance and flow structures using OpenFAST and high-fidelity CFD modeling across various operating regimes.

Complex Moving-Geometry CFD & SPH

Parallel adaptive weakly-compressible SPH for flapping airfoils. Comparison between grid-based and particle-based methods.
