Education & Curriculum

Specialized modules in Thermofluid Sciences and Renewable Energy Systems.

CFD

CFD with OpenFOAM

Numerical methods, discretization, and solver development using OpenFOAM for incompressible and compressible flows.

HRE

Hybrid Renewable Energy

Design and optimization of integrated Solar-Wind systems, including energy storage and techno-economic analysis.

AD

Wind Turbine AD

Aerodynamic performance of HAWT and VAWT, including BEM theory, wake modeling, and fluid-structure interaction.

PY

Fluid Mechanics

Foundational fluid dynamics paired with Python-based numerical modeling for flow visualization and data analysis.

TP

Turbomachinery & Propulsion

Analysis of pumps, turbines, and compressors, along with basic principles of jet and rocket propulsion systems.
